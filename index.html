Dangers of Plagiarism

By CJ Hall

Why It's Wrong

Oxford University states plagiarism is "Presenting work or ideas from another source as your own ... by incorporating it into your work without full acknowledgement." When you plagiarize, you are simply lying. You are essentially saying, "Hey, I did all of this work on my own," when in fact, no you did not. It is unethical and in academics, will fail your assignment.
